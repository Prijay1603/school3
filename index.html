  • solar tree finished.

    Currently we are awaiting the electrical wiring completion of the tree, which will enable us to attach a Solar BBQ and deliver a completely off-grid clean energy system. The system consists of 6 solar panels collecting solar energy as well as driving area lighting, facial recognition security cameras with back to base monitoring, BBQ heating elements for 10-hours continuous use at up to 300 degrees heat. All of this is capable of being viewed and monitored via smart phone apps as an educational tool allowing the monitoring of all aspects of energy capture, storage and consumption right down to component by component checks and maintenance warnings i.e when the BBQ grease trap is full and in need of cleaning. This initiative was co- sponsored by the ACT Government and EVT Energy and we will be officially launching the Solar Tree with the ACT Government in Term One of 2021.
